Kai: Lost Waters

Trailer

Description

'Kai: Lost Waters' is a 2D puzzle game where you help Kai master the power of water as they travel through a mysterious cavern. Use your mouse to move water however you like and solve puzzles by powering technology belonging to an ancient civilization.

Development Process

This was a project developed over ~6 weeks by a group of 5 for EECS 494 Project 3 at The University of Michigan.

Our initial game plan involved the player being able to control 4 elements: Fire, Water, Air, and Earth. In this first week, I worked on the fire element. I created two fire abilities and interactable torches.

In the following week, we pivoted to focus solely on water as this was our strongest mechanic. Other major project changes involved using a tilemap to create our game scenes and scaling down the level size significantly to focus on player guidance.

Contributions

Throughout the project, I worked heavily on level design and creation, puzzle design, I created the title screen, and I created the credits scene.

Additionally, I spent a lot of time in team meetings contributing to decision on game direction and project management

Technology Used
  • Unity
  • C#
  • Jira

Mr. Frosty Man

Description

Mr. Frosty Man is a platformer where the player can create their own platforms using a water gun and a magic ice wand.

Development Process

This was a solo project developed over ~1.5 weeks as a rapid prottype for EECS 494 Project 2 at The University of Michigan.

In the first phase of this project, p2_goldspike, I worked on what I believed would be the hardest feature: the water gun. I developed the first level, player movement, water gun, ice wand, freezing mechanic, a simple UI, and the ice pick.

In the second phase of this project, p2_gold, I took some feedback into account and worked on a tutorial level to better guide players and reworked the control scheme. I also created a main menu that locks levels until the player has completed the previous one, a controls menu, added music, added sound effects, created three new levels, and created fire enemies.

  • C#
